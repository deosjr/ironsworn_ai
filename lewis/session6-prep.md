# Session 6 Preparation — Hammarby

## Where We Left Off
Lewis leaves the estate for the forest northeast, seven days after receiving Saoirse's message. The queen's surveyor is active two baronies south and closing. Theodor is managing the estate alone.

Momentum: 8. Health: 4. Spirit: 4. Supply: 3. Experience: 1.

---

## The Shape of This Session

Three parts: the journey to the tree, the fight, and the return. The return ends with the surveyor already at the estate — Lewis walks in with a cut hand and no explanation.

---

## The Journey — Undertake a Journey (Dangerous)

Three waypoints before Reach Your Destination.

**Waypoint 1 — The Spring**
Familiar ground. Lewis has been here before. He stops, drinks, doesn't kneel. A rest beat. The spring is quiet — whatever it asked of him last time, it got.

**Waypoint 2 — Conlaoch**
Somewhere between the spring and the tree. Present without explanation — following or waiting, deliberately ambiguous. He tells Lewis something about the tree:
- The natives have a name for it Lewis won't be able to pronounce
- The thing bound there is older than the conquest, was deliberately contained, and the Harrying disturbed the containing
- It responds to blood because blood is the only thing it recognises as an offering — not violence, just presence

He lets Lewis pass without obstruction. No sealed entrance this time. That is the progress between them.

**Waypoint 3 — Something Already Wrong**
Dead animals, arranged too neatly. Killed grass in a circle. The air has the quality of the north field before it was sealed. Lewis knows what this means now, which is almost worse than not knowing. The thin place is already leaking.

---

## The Tree

Too large. Wrong species for this forest. The bark marked in a way that might be natural and isn't. Animals don't shelter near it. The roots have grown through something old, cracked it open.

Saoirse is there. She explains what Lewis must do: cut his hand, let the blood fall on the roots. The Haunt will respond to the offering. She will close the thin place while he holds it. He will not know when she finishes — only that the Haunt will dissolve when she does.

She cannot tell him how long it will take.

---

## The Fight — Haunt (Formidable)

- 1 progress per harm
- 3 harm per hit on Lewis
- Does not respond to Iron in the conventional sense — it is not physical. Attacks Spirit as well as Health.
- Strike/Clash rolls use Heart rather than Iron: endurance and will, not technique. Swordmaster does not apply. Veteran may.
- Face Danger to enter — the blood offering draws it out.

Lewis goes in with Health 4, Spirit 4. Two bad hits ends this badly.

The fight ends narratively when Saoirse closes the thin place — mechanically when Lewis takes the End the Fight move successfully. A miss on End the Fight means the Haunt is not yet destroyed and Lewis pays a price.

---

## The Return

Lewis returns to the estate with a bandaged hand and whatever the fight cost him. He finds the surveyor already installed — probably given the hall's main room, Theodor in quiet attendance.

The surveyor's name: **Oswin**. Arrived from the south. Queen's mandate to inventory estates and assess the region. Professionally suspicious. Has been forming impressions for however long Lewis was in the forest.

Lewis walks in at a disadvantage: visibly injured, no time to prepare, no buffer. He cannot use Shadow to manage this — he has Shadow 1 and won't try. He gives Oswin something closer to the truth than the mandate prepared him for, carefully chosen.

The first conversation with Oswin ends the session.

---

## Oswin

See campaign-setup.md for full character notes. Key points for this session:
- Condescending toward a border lord with a ruined hall — initially
- Arrived already unsettled by things seen on the road that don't fit the queen's political framing
- His mandate is the queen's shadow war against the druids; he is looking for evidence of druidic activity
- Theodor has been professionally unhelpful — answering what is asked, volunteering nothing
- The moment Oswin recalibrates is where the relationship actually begins; it will not happen this session
